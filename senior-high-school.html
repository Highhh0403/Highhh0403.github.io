STEM
The San Felipe Neri Catholic School offers the STEM strand (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics). It aims to improve the students' critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation, preparing students for careers in various jobs that require innovative thinking and technical experience. This strand emphasizes a strong foundation in both theoretical and practical thinking, allowing students to reach a higher education or professional paths in STEM-related fields.

ABM
The San Felipe Neri Catholic School Curriculum offers the Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) strand, which is an academic track to get students prepared for college and business-related careers. Its main purpose is to provide students a solid idea in accounting, business, and management concepts while pursuing their ability to think critically, analyze information, and solve problems. Fundamentals of Accounting, Business, and Management, Business Mathematics, Applied Economics, Business Finance, Organization and Management, and Principles of Marketing are among the main courses covered in the curriculum. These courses aim to give students a comprehensive grasp of financial management, commercial operations, and ethical issues, enabling them to successfully negotiate the difficulties in the business field. The ABM strand plays a crucial role in emphasizing financial literacy, assisting students in mastering accounting systems, managing business finances, creating financial statements, and spotting market trends. Important aspects of company operations are also included in the curriculum, such as comprehending management procedures and creating business plans that promote the expansion and prosperity of organizations.
