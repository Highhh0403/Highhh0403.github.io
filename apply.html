Guidelines for Applying

  • If your children are aged 3-5 years old, they are eligible to apply for Grade School level.
For Online Applications, please fill up the following information:
  • Student's Full Name
  • LRN
  • Basic personal information of the student (e.g. date of birth, age)
  • Grade Level
  • Enrolment Type
  • Parent’s/ Guardian Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Current Address
  • Mode of Payment
For Face-to-Face Enrollment, prepare the following:
  • 2 pcs 2x2 Picture (white background)
  • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate
  • Original Good Moral Certificate
  • Photocopy of Baptismal Certificate
  • Original FORM 138/ Report Card
