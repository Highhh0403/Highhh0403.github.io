Mission-Vision and Identity
Vision
A people called by the Father in Jesus Christ to become a Community of persons with Fullness of Life witnessing to the Kingdom of God by living the Paschal Mystery in the power of the Holy Spirit with Mary as Companion.
Mission
Inspired by the virtues of St. Philip Neri, we commit ourselves to:
  • Live the Gospel values;
  • Offer relevant student-centered curriculum in preparation for the world of work and life;
  • Nurture the multiple gifts and talents of the students;
  • Provide efficient and effective services and adequate facilities in support of curriculum and instruction;
  • Intensify participation in community outreach programs and church-sponsored activities;
  • Actively collaborate with parents, alumni and other stakeholders; and
  • Uphold and support directions set by RCAM-ES and Manila Ecclesiastical Province School System Association.

Core Values
Amity
by treating one another as a friend and a member of our own family (John 15:13 "There is no greater love than this to give one's life for his friends. And you are my friends if you do what I command you").
Patience
by understanding the limitations and shortcomings of the members of the academic community: administrators, faculty, students, and staff (Romans 8:25 "But if we hope for what we do not see, we wait for it with patience").
Obedience
by respecting school policies and rules, and strictly observing procedures and regulations (Psalm 112:1 "Happy is the person who honors the Lord who takes pleasure in obeying his commands").
Service
through volunterism and sharing, and by "walking an extra mile without counting the cost" (Matthew 20:28 "Just as the Son of man came not be to be served but to serve and to give. His life a ransom for many").
Trustworthiness
by believing in the worth of each member (Psalm 111:7 "The works of his hands are faithful and just; all his precepts are trustworthy").
Loyalty
to the institution and not to a person (Matthew 7:24 "No one can serve two masters"), (Sirach 6:14 "A loyal friend is like a safe shelter; Find one and you have a treasure").
Excellence
in everything we do "hindi p'wede" ang "p'wede na" in teaching, in research, in projects and assignments (Isa 28:29 "This also come from the Lord fo hists, he is wonderful in counsel and excellent in wisdom").
Simplicity
in our actions and behavior, and in what we have and aspire to have (Psalm 116:6 "The Lord saves the simple; he saved me when I was humble").
Humility
to accept our own worth, talents, gifts, and capabilities, and recognizing the worth of talents, gifts, and capabilities of others (Matthew 23:11 "Whoever exalts himself will be humbeled and who ever humbles himself will be exalted").
Integrity
by being honest in thoughts, in words, and in deeds (Proverbs 19:1 "Better a poor man who walks in his integrity than he who is crooked in his ways and rich").
Prayerfulness
doing all our actions for the glory of GOD (Philippians 4:6 "Don't worry about anything, but in all your prayers ask God for what you need, always asking him with a thankful heart").
Facilities
School Quadrangle
The quadrangle serves as a central gathering space where students assemble for flag ceremonies, school events, and recreational activities.
Plaza Filipo
Plaza Filipo is the main entry and exit point of San Felipe Neri Catholic School, serving as a gathering space where students arrive, leave, and wait for their pickups in a safe environment.
School Clinic
The clinic is a safe and accessible facility where students receive medical care, first aid, and health consultations when needed.
School Canteen
The canteen is a lively and welcoming space where students can buy and enjoy meals, snacks, and refreshments while socializing during breaks.
School Gymnasium
The school gymnasium is a spacious, multi-purpose facility designed for physical education classes, sports activities, and school events.
School Auditorium
The auditorium is a large venue where students gather for school programs, activities, and other important events.
School library
The library is where students can read, study, and access a wide range of books and learning materials.
Audio Visual Room
The audio-visual room is a multimedia-equipped space used for educational discussions, presentations, and other media-based activites.
Speech Laboratory
The Speech Laboratory provides students with a dedicated space to develop their public speaking, communication, and language skills through guided practice and learning.
Computer Laboratory
The computer laboratory is a tech-equipped learning space where students develop digital skills, conduct research, and work on technology-related projects.
Physics Laboratory
The physics lab provides students with hands-on experience in scientific experiments, enhancing their understanding of fundamental physics concepts through practical applications.
Biology Laboratory
The biology laboratory provides students with hands-on learning experiences, allowing them to explore scientific concepts through experiments and observations.
Kitchen
The kitchen is fully equipped and designed for hands-on culinary lessons, food preparation, and cooking activities.
Function Room
The function room is designed for practical lessons, and hands-on training activities.
Chapel
The chapel is a place for prayer, reflection, and spiritual activities, supporting the school's Catholic identity.
School Swimming Pool
The swimming pool is used for swimming lessons, helping students develop essential water safety and swimming skills.
