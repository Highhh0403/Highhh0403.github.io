Junior High School
San Felipe Neri Catholic School (SFNCS) is a private institution that offers a nurturing and holistic educational environment for its students. The junior high school department at SFNCS provides a well-rounded, faith-based education designed to guide young adolescents through their developmental years, helping them grow academically, socially, and spiritually. Senior High School
The Senior High School department for San Felipe Neri Catholic School (SFNCS) is committed to effectively cultivate the personal and academic skills of its students. Our Senior High School department offers a variety of strands to pick from, including Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM), Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS), Accountancy, Business, and Management (ABM) Technical-Vocational-Livelihood - Information and Communications Technology (TVL - ICT), Technical-Vocational-Livelihood - Cookery (TVL - Cookery), and Technical-Vocational-Livelihood - Tourism (TVL-Tourism). This ensures that the students find the right academic place for their skills. The SFNCS Senior High School department incorporates both conceptual and hands-on learning experiences which allows students to apply them into practical situations, appropriate to their strands. The SFNCS building proudly features a variety of facilities, including the Computer Laboratory, Biology Laboratory, Chemistry Laboratory, and more, which enhance student engagement with their respective strands. Additionally, SFNCS provides focused work immersion and research opportunities tailored to each strand, allowing students to gain real-world experience and deepen their expertise in their chosen fields.
